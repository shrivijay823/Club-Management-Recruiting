Requirements

 Prerequisite:

  1. no backlogs
  2. cgpa>8
  3. suitable profile for the post
  4. A candidate is selected based on questions answered followed by an interview. The interview will be panelled by the office bearers as well as the faculty in charge.

Posting Available:

  • Vice President  (For Final years)
  • Secretary (For Pre Final years
  • Treasurer (For Pre Final years)
  • Joint-Secretary (For Sophomore)
  • Senior Executive Member (For Sophomore)
  • Executive Member (For Sophomore)
Post Description:
  • Vice President

       A person who manages the entire crew of the club as well as the events that are conducted.A Vice President's duties as a social club officer are similar to that of the President's duties. Presides at meetings in the absence of the president.

    • Plans, coordinates, and recruits committees to manage a series of meetings and programs
    • Coordinates programs with the president and the executive committee
    • Provides timely and interesting advance information for newsletters and mailings
    • Provides or coordinates information on forthcoming events to the secretary for inclusion in meeting notices, newsletters or electronic distribution lists.

  • Secretary

       A person who is part of the office of the club who works over the events that are conducted. It is important to note that although the Secretary ensures that these responsibilities are met, much of the work may be delegated to paid staff or volunteers.

    • Prepare the agenda for club/group meetings in consultation with the Chairperson.
    • Make arrangements including venue, date, times and hospitality for club meetings.
    • Send adequate notice of the meetings.
    • Collect and collate reports from office bearers.
    • Take the minutes of meetings.
    • Write up the minutes as soon as possible after the meeting.

  • Treasurer

       A person who looks after the funds that are involved in the club The Treasurer is responsible for the managing the flow of money into the club and for allocating and recording how that money is spent to run the club.The treasurer also works in conjunction with the chairperson in planning the development of the club.

  • Joint-Secretary

      A person who works with the secretary in all aspects of the club.Ensuring the smooth functioning of the club by giving enough support to the board.

  • Senior Executive Member

       A person who takes care of the day to day tasks.Collaborates with other members of the executive committee and board of directors to create and execute exciting, interesting events for the club constituency.Works closely with secretary and Excecutive members to promote upcoming events

  • Executive Member

       A person who volunteers in the events that are organized.Publicizes club activities through email, social media and the club website.
